AI Orchestration & Multi-LLM Workflows

Prompteus makes AI adoption simple, reliable, and compliant for modern teams on Google Cloud.

Solution Overview

Prompteus is a full-stack AI orchestration platform that enables teams to design, automate, and govern complex multi-LLM workflows with confidence. Built on Google Cloud, the platform connects structured logic, human-in-the-loop decisions, and model flexibility with enterprise-grade security and compliance.

By integrating with Vertex AI, Cloud Run, BigQuery, and IAM, Prompteus standardizes how AI is used across products and operationsensuring consistent outputs, auditable decisions, and cost-efficient execution at scale.

Vertex AI Cloud Run BigQuery IAM & SSO Multi-LLM Routing Human-in-the-Loop

Key Capabilities

  • Design and orchestrate end-to-end AI workflows with branching, validations, and reusable templates.
  • Route requests across multiple LLMs and SLMs for optimal cost, latency, or accuracy.
  • Keep humans in the loop for review, approvals, and exception handling.
  • Run repeatable flows for document processing, knowledge retrieval, classification, and summarization.
  • Enforce security, access control, and auditability across high-stakes environments.

Who This Is For

Prompteus is ideal for organizations that need both flexibility and governance:

  • Software & internet companies scaling AI across products and teams.
  • Financial services and healthcare organizations operating in regulated environments.
  • Professional services firms building knowledge-centric AI assistants.
  • Public sector and enterprises requiring traceable, explainable AI workflows.
